The global robotic vacuum market is projected to reach $45.2B by 2029, growing at 23.5% CAGR. 
Key drivers include increasing consumer adoption of smart home technology, rising labor costs, 
and improved AI navigation systems. Market leaders include iRobot (35% share), Dyson (18% share), 
and emerging players focusing on commercial applications.

Regional Analysis:
- North America: $12.5B (2024), driven by high disposable income
- Europe: $8.9B (2024), emphasis on energy efficiency
- Asia-Pacific: $15.2B (2024), largest growth potential

Technology Trends:
- AI-powered navigation and mapping
- Integration with smart home ecosystems
- Sustainable materials and energy efficiency
- Multi-surface cleaning capabilities
